Generate a built-in generic rules bundle under `.mycodemap/rules/`, detect drift on repeat init, show update previews, and emit copy-paste AI context snippets without auto-editing user-owned `CLAUDE.md` or `AGENTS.md`.

Purpose: AI guardrails should become a clear project asset without taking ownership of the user's existing context files.
Output: Rules helper, built-in templates, receipt/manual-action integration, and focused tests.
</objective>
